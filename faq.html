Frequently Asked Questions




What is the 'Taylor Swift Times?'

Taylor Swift Times is a fanpage dedicated to the one and only Taylor Swift. We provide fellow swifties with the latest and greatest on our famous global superstar. We specialize in bringing you news as soon as we find it, and providing the point of view of superfans in our articles.

How do I view your articles?

Easy! Just go to the navigation bar on the top and go to 'Articles'. They are listed in chronological order.

How can I contact you?

You can contact us by going to the navigation bar on the top and going to 'Contact'. There, you can send us an email!
